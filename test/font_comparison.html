Go Sans-Serif Font Implementation

This browser now uses the Go fonts - high-quality, proportional, sans-serif fonts from the Go project.

Font Styles

Normal text: The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog. 0123456789

Bold text: The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog. 0123456789

Italic text: The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog. 0123456789

Bold Italic: The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog. 0123456789

Font Sizes

Small text (12px): Clear and readable even at small sizes!

Normal text (14px): Perfect for body text and content.

Large text (20px): Great for headings and emphasis.

Features

  • Proportional spacing (not monospaced)
  • Anti-aliased rendering
  • Native bold and italic variants
  • Built-in to Go distribution
  • No system font dependencies
